  1. The place where I want to live in the future.
  2. The place where I live.

Now, it's your turn:

Draw a star and share with me five things about yourself. You may include:

Warm up

Tell me about yourself.

  1. Where are you from?
    1. Example: I am from Toluca.
  2. What do you do?
    1. Example: I am an illustrator.
  3. What is your nationality?
    1. Example: I am Mexican.
  4. What is your hobby?
    1. Example: I like to read sci-fi books.
  5. Do you like animals?
    1. Example: Yes, I love dogs.

Read the dialogue:

  1. Ana: Hello, are you lost?
  2. Ben: Hi! Yes, I am. I am a new student at the Arts Insitute.
  3. Ana: Where are you from?
  4. Ben: I am from Mexico.
  5. Ana: Where do you want to go?
  6. Ben: I am hungry. I am looking for a restaurant.
  7. Ana: My friends are in an Italian restaurant around the corner. Do you want to eat with us?
  8. Ben: Yes, I would love to. You are so nice!
  9. Ana: All right, let's go. What is your name?
  10. Ben: My name is Ben. I am 20 years old.
